Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

[ВАШЕ ІМ'Я/КОМПАНІЯ] ("Company", "we", "our", "us") provides the PDF Scanner mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we process, why we process it, and your choices.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to:

  • The iOS application PDF Scanner
  • Support communication channels (email)
  • Subscription and billing flows processed through Apple App Store / RevenueCat

2. Core Principle

Your scanned documents and extracted OCR text are processed on-device.

We do not upload document contents to our servers. All document handling is private and stays local to your iPhone unless you explicitly choose to export it.

3. Data We Process

3.1 Data stored locally on your device

  • Scanned images and generated PDFs
  • OCR text extracted from your documents
  • Folder structure, edit history, and in-app settings

3.2 Subscription and transaction data

When you purchase Premium, Apple and RevenueCat may process:

  • Product identifier and purchase status
  • Subscription renewal/expiration state
  • Anonymous app user identifier

3.3 Diagnostics and product analytics

We may process technical telemetry such as app version, device model, and crash diagnostics to ensure stability and improve UX.

3.4 Support data

If you contact support, we process your email address and message content to resolve your issues.

4. Notifications

If you opt in, we may send local notifications. You can disable these anytime in iOS Settings.

5. Legal Basis

We process data under: Contract performance, Legitimate interests, and Consent (for notifications/optional analytics).

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share limited data only with essential service providers:

  • Apple: Payments and App Store infrastructure
  • RevenueCat: Subscription state verification

7. Tracking and Advertising

We do not use cross-app tracking for third-party advertising unless explicitly consented via Apple ATT prompt.

8. Data Retention

  • Local document data remains on your device until you delete it.
  • Operational records are retained only as long as needed for billing and compliance.

9. Security

We apply reasonable technical measures to protect your data, acknowledging that no storage method is 100% secure.

10. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you have rights to access, correction, deletion, and portability of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

12. International Transfers

Data is processed with appropriate safeguards under applicable laws.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date.
